# Business Requirements

## Business Goals
The platform must help the organization deliver software faster, with higher confidence and stronger compliance controls.

## Business Requirements
1. Provide a repeatable and automated software delivery process.
2. Reduce operational risk by enforcing policy and security checks.
3. Improve team productivity through self-service capabilities.
4. Enable traceability from requirements to deployment and monitoring.
5. Support audit and governance requirements with documented controls.

## Expected Business Outcomes
- Faster release cycles
- Lower support and incident overhead
- Better alignment between development and operations
- Stronger regulatory and internal compliance posture
